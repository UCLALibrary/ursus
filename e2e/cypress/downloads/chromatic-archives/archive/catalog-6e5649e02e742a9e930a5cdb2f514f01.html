Santa Monica coastline from the Palisades Park cliffs at Montana Avenue, Santa Monica, 1946

Description:
Photograph of the Santa Monica coastline from the Palisades Park cliffs, looking south. At foreground, a large palm tree stands across from rock cliffs at left, a portion of the park’s rustic eucalyptus branch fence and trees in view. Below, a stretch of Pacific Coast Highway (California Highway 1), with cars lining the road and parked in lots near what are presumably beach homes, clubs and businesses. These structures follow along the Highway and beach- the beach populated by crowds on the sand and in the water. At center sits the Santa Monica Pier and Yacht Harbor. Beyond the pier, the coastline continues into the distance. Photographed at the foot of Montana Avenue, above the Sorrento Beach Club.
